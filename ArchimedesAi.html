This is Arcimedes

Welcome to the centre of company, our main product. Arcimedes, the best Ai going

What is Arcimedes?

In basic all Arcimedes is, is an Artifical Intelligence, and that's what he is seen as by everyone. But we see Arcimedes as the future of Ai, the next ground breaking revolution in Artifical Intelligence.

What can it do?

The possibilties of Arcimedes are infinte. The main goal with it, was to make it drive a car completely on it's own and safely get people from A to B. but as the project moved through different stages, we began to broaded the what it could do. We gave it a changable name and identity, so it's Arcimedes to us, but to you it could be anything. He is built with machine learning, so the things you do, Arcimedes will learn and copy them. He will keep you up to date with the lates news, weather, traffic, health. He'll even monitor your car checking to make sure you have enough fuel or power in your car. He's your personal assistant no matter when or where you are, he comes along with you.

Why is it a revolution?

We believe it's a revoltion because no one has yet attempted what we're planning on doing with Ai, yes we have Siri and cortana on our phones and computers. But they don't sync with each other, they don't share data about you with each other and learn about you. Arcimedes does it all, we know that he will serve regardless
